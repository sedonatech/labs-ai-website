## Why People Compare ElevenLabs and Labs AI

ElevenLabs is the gold standard for AI voice quality. When people discover Labs AI, the first question is often: does it sound as good as ElevenLabs? The answer is yes, because Labs AI is powered by the same ElevenLabs voice technology.

## ElevenLabs: What It Does Well

ElevenLabs is a professional AI voice platform used by studios, agencies, and developers. It offers:

- **Best-in-class voice quality**: the most natural AI voices available
- **Voice cloning**: create a voice model from a short audio sample
- **API access**: integrate voices into apps and workflows
- **Large voice library**: hundreds of voices across styles and languages
- **Custom voice design**: build voices from scratch

ElevenLabs is a serious tool for serious users. It is powerful, flexible, and widely used in professional production.

## ElevenLabs: The Limitations for iPhone Users

Despite its quality, ElevenLabs has real friction for mobile users:

- **No native iOS app**: you must use a browser on your phone
- **Not designed for mobile**: the web interface is built for desktop
- **Expensive**: free tier is very limited; paid plans start at $5/month and go much higher
- **Requires account setup**: you cannot generate audio instantly without registration
- **Desktop workflow**: integrates better with professional desktop setups than mobile creation

## Labs AI: ElevenLabs Quality on iPhone

Labs AI was built to solve exactly this problem. It takes ElevenLabs voice technology and wraps it in a native iPhone experience.

What Labs AI offers:

- **Same ElevenLabs voices**: identical voice quality, built on the same technology
- **Native iPhone app**: designed specifically for iOS, fast and intuitive
- **Voice cloning**: clone your voice directly from your iPhone
- **100+ voices**: full access to a wide voice library
- **50+ languages**: generate content in any major language
- **Free to start**: download and use without a paid subscription
- **No browser needed**: everything happens in the app
