## What makes a great text-to-speech app?

Before comparing apps, here's what actually matters:

- **Voice quality**: does it sound natural, or robotic?
- **Language selection**: can it handle the languages you need?
- **Voice variety**: can you pick a style that fits your content?
- **Voice cloning**: can it sound like *you*?
- **Ease of use**: can you go from script to audio in under 30 seconds?
