**TL;DR:** Speechify and Labs AI are built for different things. Speechify is a listening app that reads documents and articles to you. Labs AI is a creation app that generates professional voiceovers, podcast audio, and video narration. If you want to create audio content on iPhone, Labs AI is the better choice.

## What Speechify Does Well

Speechify is one of the most popular productivity apps for consuming written content. You paste a document, article, or PDF, and it reads it back to you at high speed. It is designed for people who want to absorb information faster by listening rather than reading.

Key strengths of Speechify:

- **Speed listening**: adjustable playback from 1x to 4.5x speed
- **Document import**: works with PDFs, web articles, Google Docs
- **Study tool**: useful for students and heavy readers
- **Cross-platform**: available on iOS, Android, Mac, and Chrome

## What Labs AI Does Well

Labs AI is built for a completely different use case: creating audio content. Instead of reading your content back to you, it generates studio-quality voiceovers that you can use in videos, podcasts, social content, and more.

Key strengths of Labs AI:

- **100+ AI voices**: professional voices across every style and tone
- **50+ languages**: create content for global audiences
- **Voice cloning**: generate audio that sounds like you, without recording
- **Content creation focused**: built for creators, not listeners
- **Native iOS app**: designed specifically for iPhone
- **Free to get started**: no credit card required

## The Core Difference

Speechify answers the question: "How do I consume more content faster?"

Labs AI answers the question: "How do I create professional audio content without a microphone?"

These are fundamentally different tools. If you are a creator making YouTube videos, TikToks, podcasts, or voice content, Labs AI is the right choice. If you want an app to read your emails and PDFs to you while commuting, Speechify is the right choice.

## Price Comparison

**Speechify**: free tier is very limited. Full access requires Speechify AI at around $139/year.

**Labs AI**: free to download on the App Store with access to voices and core features. No subscription required to get started.

## Voice Quality

Both apps use AI voices, but they serve different purposes. Speechify optimizes for clarity at high speeds. Labs AI optimizes for naturalness and expressiveness in recorded content, which is why it uses ElevenLabs technology for premium voice quality.

## Which One Should You Choose?

Choose **Speechify** if you want to listen to documents, articles, and books faster.

Choose **Labs AI** if you want to create voiceovers, podcasts, video narration, or any audio content on your iPhone.

Most creators end up with both: Speechify for consuming research, Labs AI for producing content.
