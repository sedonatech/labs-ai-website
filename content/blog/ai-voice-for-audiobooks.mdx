Before converting your text to audio, ensure your manuscript is properly formatted:

- Remove any special formatting that might confuse the AI reader
- Break longer chapters into manageable sections (5,000-10,000 words per section)
- Check for spelling errors and proper punctuation
- Add natural pauses where dramatic effect is needed
