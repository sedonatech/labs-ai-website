## You Do Not Need to Record Your Voice to Start a Podcast

The biggest barrier to starting a podcast is usually the same: people do not want to hear themselves talk, they do not have a decent microphone, or they simply feel uncomfortable recording their own voice. AI voice generation removes that barrier entirely.

With a good **AI podcast voice generator**, you write your script, choose a voice, and get broadcast-quality audio in seconds. No studio, no mic, no awkward takes.

This guide walks you through exactly how to do it using [Labs AI](https://apps.apple.com/be/app/labs-ai-text-to-speech/id6741198402), a text-to-speech app for iPhone.

## Step 1: Define Your Podcast Format

Before you open any app, decide on the basics:

- **Topic**: what is your podcast about? Keep it specific enough to attract a defined audience.
- **Format**: solo narration, interview-style (with a second AI voice), or storytelling.
- **Episode length**: aim for 5 to 10 minutes to start. That is 700 to 1,500 words of script.

## Step 2: Write Your Script

AI voices work best when given clean, well-written text. Write conversationally, as if you are speaking directly to one person. Short sentences sound better when converted to speech than long, complex ones.

A simple episode structure that works well:

- **Hook** (30 seconds): one sharp sentence that makes someone want to keep listening
- **Context** (1 to 2 minutes): what is the topic and why does it matter today
- **Main content** (3 to 5 minutes): your core points, tips, or story
- **Outro** (30 seconds): a call to action or a teaser for the next episode

## Step 3: Generate the Voice with Labs AI

Download [Labs AI](https://apps.apple.com/be/app/labs-ai-text-to-speech/id6741198402) from the App Store. It is free.

Once inside the app:

1. Paste your script into the text field
2. Browse the voice library, which includes 100+ voices across 50+ languages
3. Pick a voice that fits your show's tone. A news-style podcast works well with a clear, authoritative voice. A lifestyle or wellness show benefits from something warmer and more relaxed.
4. Generate the audio

The output sounds natural, not robotic. That matters because listeners will tune out within seconds if a voice sounds artificial.

## Step 4: Add Music and Export

Once you have your voiceover file, bring it into a free editing tool like GarageBand (available on iOS) or a desktop editor. Layer in a short intro music clip, your generated voiceover, and a simple outro. Export as an MP3.

## Step 5: Publish

Upload your episode to a podcast hosting platform like Anchor, Buzzsprout, or Spotify for Podcasters. Most have free tiers. Your episode will be distributed to Apple Podcasts, Spotify, and Google Podcasts automatically.
