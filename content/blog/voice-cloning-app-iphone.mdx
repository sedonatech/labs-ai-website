## What is voice cloning?

Voice cloning is the process of training an AI model on a sample of your voice, so it can reproduce your unique voice characteristics when reading any text.

The result: you paste a script, tap Generate, and get back audio that sounds exactly like you: without recording a single word.

## How to clone your voice with Labs AI

### Step 1: Download Labs AI

Get **Labs AI** free from the App Store. Open the app and navigate to the Voice Cloning section.

### Step 2: Record your voice sample

You only need a short recording: about 30-60 seconds of clear speech. Tips for a good sample:

- Find a quiet room
- Speak at your normal pace and tone
- Read a paragraph or two naturally
- Avoid background noise

### Step 3: Let the AI clone your voice

Labs AI sends your recording to its AI engine, which analyzes your voice and creates a model. This takes just a few seconds.

### Step 4: Generate audio from any script

Once your voice is cloned, paste any script and tap Generate. The AI produces audio in your cloned voice: as many times as you want, for any content.
