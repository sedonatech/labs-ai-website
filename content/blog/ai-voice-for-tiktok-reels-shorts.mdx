## The anatomy of a high-performing short-form video

Most viral short-form videos follow this structure:

1. **Hook** (0-3 seconds): a bold claim or shocking statement
2. **Setup** (3-10 seconds): context for the hook
3. **Payoff** (10-30 seconds): the actual content
4. **CTA** (last 2 seconds): follow for more, link in bio, etc.

Write your script with this in mind. Short sentences. One idea per line. Your AI voiceover will land harder.

## How to create a TikTok voiceover with Labs AI

### Step 1: Write a tight script

Keep it under 150 words for a 30-60 second video. Read it out loud first to check the rhythm. If it sounds awkward out loud, it'll sound awkward in the AI voice too.

### Step 2: Pick the right voice style

In Labs AI, you have 100+ voices to choose from. For short-form video:

- **Energetic, punchy voices** work well for motivation, finance, and listicle content
- **Calm, clear voices** work for educational and informational videos
- **Warm, storytelling voices** work for narrative and emotional content

Spend 2 minutes testing a few: it makes a big difference in retention.

### Step 3: Generate and export

Paste your script in Labs AI, pick your language, and tap Generate. Export the audio file and import it into your video editor.
