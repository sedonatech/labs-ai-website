**TL;DR:** To make YouTube videos without recording your voice, use Labs AI to generate a professional AI voiceover from your script. Export the audio, add it to your footage in any video editor, and publish. No microphone or recording setup needed.

Faceless YouTube channels are one of the fastest-growing formats in 2025. The concept is simple: you create videos without ever appearing on camera or recording your own voice. Instead, you use **AI voiceovers** to narrate your content.

Here's exactly how to do it with **Labs AI**.

## Why faceless YouTube channels work

- No camera needed
- No recording setup
- No need to be "on" all the time
- You can produce content at scale
- Topics like finance, history, science, and listicles all work perfectly

## Step 1: Write your script

Write your video script like a blog post. Aim for 800-1500 words for a 5-10 minute video. Use short paragraphs and clear sentences: they convert to speech better.

## Step 2: Open Labs AI

Download **Labs AI** from the App Store (free). Open the app and go to the voice selection screen.

## Step 3: Choose your voice

Pick a voice that matches your channel's tone:

- **News/documentary style**: neutral, authoritative voices
- **Storytelling**: warm, engaging narrators
- **Educational**: clear, paced voices
- **Meditation/relaxation**: soft, calm voices

Labs AI has 100+ voices across these styles. Take 2 minutes to test a few with a sample sentence.

## Step 4: Select your language

Labs AI supports 50+ languages. If your channel targets a specific region or language, you can generate native-sounding voiceovers without hiring a voice actor.

## Step 5: Paste your script and generate

Paste your script into the app. Tap **Generate**. In a few seconds, you have a studio-quality audio file ready to use in your video editor.

## Step 6: Add to your video

Import the audio into your video editor (CapCut, DaVinci Resolve, iMovie, Premiere). Add B-roll footage, stock videos, or screen recordings on top.

## Pro tip: Use voice cloning for consistency

If you're building a channel with a recognizable voice, use Labs AI's voice cloning feature. Record yourself once, clone your voice, and every future video uses that same voice: without you ever having to record again.
